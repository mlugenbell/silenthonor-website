Follow-up check-ins to track progress and adjust as your life changes.
Prepare for Your Session

Before your first session, it helps to have the following ready:

→ Recent pay stubs or income statements
→ List of all debts (credit cards, loans, etc.)
→ Your current monthly expenses (rough estimate is fine)
→ Your 3 biggest financial goals
→ A free copy of your credit report from AnnualCreditReport.com
